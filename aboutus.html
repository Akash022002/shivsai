    About Akash Group

    Naminath Group of Companies is a construction & Infrastructure
    conglomerate with vast experience & a focus on development. SHREE
    NAMINATH GROUP has the vision to construct prime residential,
    commercial & shopping complexes at Prominent Locations. In our Endeavor
    to achieve excellence, we build landmarks of the highest quality at
    affordable prices. We aim to be part of the rapid development in Realty,
    Construction & Infrastructure in India & Globally. Commitment is our motto,
    and maintaining a delivery schedule is our goal. We have a track record of
    sustained growth & customer satisfaction. Lots of research goes on the
    feasibility, technical viability, and sound financial planning. We combine
    unique project planning with advanced techniques to provide cost-effectivev
    real estate solutions. While having a passion for achieving supreme quality
    of design and architecture, utmost care is taken to make our buildings
    environment friendly, saving on electricity and water to ensure sustainable
    development. We specialize in also picking up Sick Projects and Turning around the same.
    At Shree Naminath Group, our principal is to take utmost care of our
    investors and end-users & all people involved with us.
